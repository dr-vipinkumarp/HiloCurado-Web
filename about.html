About Me

Automotive/UAV Embedded Systems (Hardware & Software) | QT/Visual Studio/Visual Code | C/C++ Bare-Metal | Firmware/RTOS/Linux Device Driver Developer | Eagle/Altium/KiCAD PCB Designer

I have achieved Research Excellent award by InSc in 2021 and SHEN Best Researcher Awards in 2021. I'm a member of ECS(USA), CESD(Pune), IEEE(California), IAENG( Hong Kong), ATSC( Washington DC, USA), MICROCHIP(USA), IFERP(Chennai, India), MIRED(New York, USA), InSc(India) I'm a University of Mumbai PhD candidate in remote sensing. I graduated from Pune University with a master's degree in electronics and telecommunications engineering. I have nine research papers published in international journals and seven papers presented at international conferences. I have published one book on Wireless Sensor Networks in 2018. I serves as a geoscience and remote sensing instrumentation reviewer for international journals such as IEEE, T&F, and Elsevier. He has 11 years of research experience in the field of Embedded Systems. I specialises in Hardware and Software Embedded Systems, Data Analysis, Remote Sensing, Ballastic UAV and Missile Navigation, UAV Power Management, UAV and Missile Flight Management, and GPS Analysis.
